    Campus Hiring

    The Training & Placement Cell of the Institute centrally handles campus placement of the Engineering, Architecture and Pharmacy students. The Cell provides complete support to the visiting companies at every stage of the placement process. Arrangements for pre-placement talks, skill tests, interviews, and group discussions are made as per the requirement of the visiting companies.
