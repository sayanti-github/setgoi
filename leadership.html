    Sri Tapan Kr. Pobi
    The Founder President, SET

    SET is a center for growth, innovation, and excellence in addition to being a place of learning. This institution offers a diverse range of courses, including engineering, architecture, and Pharmacy in its umbrella institutions.

    We encourage innovation and problem-solving in the engineering discipline to better equip our students to meet the complex problems of the contemporary world. While our medical programs develop caring, knowledgeable healthcare workers, our architecture program promotes creative design thinking and sustainable solutions.

    Swami Vivekananda once opined: “There is no other teacher but your own soul” and inspired by this vision of Swamiji, our students stay abreast with the ethics, ethos and ideologies of a completely integrated human being. They are taught to accept their own social and cultural responsibilities.

    I urge our students to seize the opportunity that SET presents, to dream large, and to pursue excellence as we move forward. Together, we'll keep adding to the institution's success tales and change the world for the better.

    Sri Shiba Prasad Dutta
    Chairman, SETGOI

    Welcome to SETGOI!

    Enjoy the journey toward a great future. Since your present shapes your future, we tend to sharpen your present capabilities so that you can emerge as a successful engineer or entrepreneur in the future. Present day institutions must produce technically adept and bright graduates to be able to cope with the fast-changing world. To achieve holistic development of the students and education system is our utmost priority. Our students excel in all spheres of academia. Our main objective is to produce a whole bunch of robust graduates who shall, with full gusto, create benchmarks through their skills and ideas in core corporate sectors.

    SETGOI advocates equal, fair right for education and admission system for all. No discrimination, cheating, unfair actions are meted out towards our students.

    Prof. (Dr.) Saikat Chaterjee
    Director, SETGOI

    “The key to new and good ideas come from having a multidisciplinary range of interests”.

    The integrated campus of SETGOI offering programmes in multiple disciplines echoes the same idea while facilitating cross-disciplinary learning among the faculty and students. Welcome to a campus away from the hurly-burly of city life but bustling with a diverse range of campus activities. The legacy of our learned faculty, outperforming graduates, and the thriving alumni will continue to inspire every new member joining the family. Take a pause to go through the pages within our website, and explore our attempts to provide our students with a cheerful, productive and satisfying experience at all levels of their programme of studies.

    Identify the opportunities and make the best of them.

    Prof. (Dr.) Arkendu Chatterjee
    Principal, School of Pharmacy, SETGOI

    Pharmacy is one of the noblest professions in the healthcare sector, and we aim to provide education in the field of Pharmacy while currently offering the Bachelor of Pharmacy (B. Pharm.) programme approved by the Pharmacy Council of India. The fact is that the entire pharmaceutical arena in India is in a very remarkable position. Our goal is to produce well-groomed skilled professionals who would add value not only to the pharmaceutical industry but to the pharmacy profession as a whole.

    The Institution aims to provide technology-equipped classrooms, rich collections of books in the library, and well-equipped labs for practical skill building. Further highly qualified, experienced, and skilled faculty pool would guide the students transforming them from an amateur to a skilled professional. We aim to create an environment where the students, the faculty members, and the staff would jointly grow and learn, and face challenges together. I am in belief that the Institution will be successful in achieving its objectives by generating genius, talented, and globally competent pharmacists and would achieve the status of a “Centre of Excellence in Pharmaceutical Education”.

    Prof. (Dr.) Indrani Dhar
    Principal, Rani Rashmoni School of Architecture(RRSA), SETGOI
    (Architect & Town Planner) (Secretary of ITPI-WBRC, Associate member of IIA, ITPI)

    “The mother art is architecture. Without an architecture of our own we have no soul of our own civilization.” – Frank Lloyd Wright

    Architecture, the art or science of building specifically: the art or practice of designing and building structures and especially habitable ones…

    Rani Rashmoni School of architecture has become a landmark in architectural education. It's the first Private Architectural College in West Bengal. The uniqueness lies in its location, far away from the city hubbubs, within a serene green environment coupled with the state-of-the-art infrastructure and impeccably talented and experienced people gathered here. All of us nurture a different vision about architectural education, making this the strength of the institute since its inception, and one that will only get stronger and more enriched with time.

    We focus on each & every individual student, to bring out their full potential and empower them with a winning attitude. We guide them individually and nurture their inner talents to bloom as successful Architects.

    We mentor our students to implicate our values and insights into the state-of-the-art facility that nourish and amplify the students' potential and bring out their talent to mould them into the masters of tomorrow.

    Every fresh mind is encouraged to be prepared to take risks, and with our strong support they will achieve success and be a successful part and parcel of our fraternity.

    Dr. Snigdhadip Ghosh
    Registrar, SETGOI

    "One of the most powerful tools to empower individuals and communities is making certain that any individual who wants to receive a quality education can do so." With these objectives, Sanaka Educational Trust’s Group of Institutions is striving hard to provide one of the best 'Edu-Tech' supports to all students by means of implementation of Outcome Based Education System with specific inputs from industry experts, interdisciplinary research of global importance, highly qualified faculty, and active industry-academia interactions supportive infrastructure for holistic development leading to secured career. SETGOI is an educational eco-system, which consistently attempts to offer the best educational facilities to its students while evolving itself to the changing educational needs of the corporate world. We groom our students to develop into individuals with strong ethical values and also focus on enriching their social etiquettes. SETGOI aims to develop highest quality minds, which are always questing for new ideas or inventions that can serve the society. Apart from the academic facilities, the Institute strongly focuses on extracurricular activities to nurture its students. We impart the best of the knowledge to our students with unmatched extracurricular and emotional quotient related inputs and the capability to acquire knowledge, lead and excel to make them responsible human beings.
